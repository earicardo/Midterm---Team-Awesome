Santorum Suggests Praying Debris Back into Buildings

by Rupert McKnight

March 6, 2011

Presidential hopeful Rick Santorum took a side trip today from his election campaign trail to look over the recent devastation caused by powerful tornadoes that ripped through southern Illinois. Santorum toured many sites of destruction and spoke to many residents involved in the tragedy.

One such affected resident asked Mr. Santorum what help there was available to her and her family from the federal government. Mr. Santorum immediately told the woman to stop being lazy and go get a job and that the building could easily be prayed back to health if this country was not filled with so many heathens. Mr. Santorum then got on his knees and began to pray. He asked God to heal this land and bring the buildings back to their former glory.

After the prayer Mr. Santorum opened his eyes and saw nothing but debris. At this point he rose from his knees, pointed to the resident who was now staring in complete shock at the former Senator, and explained to the resident that maybe she deserved all of this since she clearly did not have the faith to keep a class five tornado from leveling her neighborhood. After this exchange Mr. Santorum sprinted to his awaiting tour bus screaming "Dear Lord, the rubble dust is burning my eyes. Let's get the [expletive] out of here!
